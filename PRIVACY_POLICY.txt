Meropaw Privacy Policy
Effective date: 2026-02-04

This Privacy Policy explains how Meropaw (the "App") collects, uses, and shares
information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

1) Information We Collect

Information you provide
- Account and contact information: email (for sign-in), and optional profile
  details such as name and phone number.
- Pet profile information: pet name, bio, breed, age or birth date, color,
  microchip number, allergies, personality traits, wellness notes, and
  achievements.
- Activities and reminders: activity type, title, notes, dates/times, time zone,
  and completion status.
- Health records and appointments: record type, title, dates, notes, vet/clinic
  details, appointment location details, and uploaded attachments (e.g., PDFs).
- Memories and media: photos or videos you upload, titles, notes, and related
  metadata (e.g., favorites, dimensions).
- Expenses and receipts: expense details (amount, category, date, notes,
  merchant) and receipt files.

Information collected automatically or stored on your device
- Device identifiers for sign-in alerts: device ID/label and first/last seen
  timestamps.
- Local app storage: preferences (e.g., cloud sync toggle), cached data to speed
  up the app, and temporary onboarding metadata. Photos and large files are not
  stored in local storage; they are uploaded to cloud storage.

2) How We Use Information
- Provide core features such as pet profiles, activity tracking, health records,
  reminders, expenses, and memories.
- Sync data across devices when cloud sync is enabled.
- Process receipts and clinical summaries to extract structured data (e.g.,
  dates, totals, clinic details) to help auto-fill records.
- Send in-app notifications about reminders, health updates, and receipts.
- Maintain account security, including new device sign-in alerts.
- Improve reliability and troubleshoot issues.

3) How We Share Information
- Cloud services: We use Supabase to host our database and store media files.
- Document processing: When you upload a receipt or clinical summary for
  automated extraction, we send the document to Google Cloud Document AI to
  extract relevant fields. We send only the document needed for the feature.
- Service providers: We may share data with trusted vendors who help us operate
  the App, under confidentiality obligations.
We do not sell your personal information.

4) Data Storage and Retention
- Your data is stored in our cloud database and storage buckets while your
  account is active or as needed to provide the App.
- You can delete records (e.g., health records, expenses, memories) within the
  App. If you request account deletion, we will remove your stored data within a
  reasonable time, subject to legal requirements.
- Local data stored on your device remains until you remove it or uninstall the
  App.

5) Security
We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. No method of
transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute
security.

6) Your Choices and Rights
- Access, update, or delete your data in the App.
- Turn off cloud sync in settings (some features may require it).
- Manage device permissions (camera, photos) in your device settings.

7) Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect
personal information from children.

8) Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date
above and may notify you in the App if changes are material.

9) Contact Us
For questions or requests, contact us using the support email listed in the
App or through the in-app support option.
